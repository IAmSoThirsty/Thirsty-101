# Open Questions

This page records questions that remain unresolved and may materially affect the work.

Questions are not defects in the documentation. Concealed uncertainty is.

## Question register

| ID | Question | Scope | Why it matters | Current evidence | Next test | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| _Q-000_ | _Unresolved question_ | _Project, concept, or system_ | _What changes depending on the answer?_ | _What is presently known?_ | _What could reduce uncertainty?_ | _Open / Investigating / Blocked / Resolved_ |

## Question record

### Q-000 — Question

**Scope:**  
Where this question applies.

**Why it matters:**  
What design, claim, implementation, or decision depends on the answer.

**Known:**  
Established observations, evidence, and constraints.

**Unknown:**  
The exact uncertainty—not a restatement of the whole problem.

**Current hypotheses:**  
Possible answers, each identified as provisional.

**Disconfirming evidence:**  
What would show that a hypothesis is wrong.

**Next useful action:**  
The smallest test, reading, implementation, comparison, or conversation likely to reduce uncertainty.

**Resolution condition:**  
What must become true before the question can be marked resolved.

## Use

The question register should expose:

- what I know I do not know;
- what I may be assuming without support;
- what must be tested before a claim becomes stronger;
- what questions connect otherwise separate projects;
- what has been deliberately deferred rather than forgotten.
