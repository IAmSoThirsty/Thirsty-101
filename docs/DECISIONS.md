# Decisions

This page records consequential choices and the reasoning that made them valid at the time.

The goal is not to prove every decision correct forever. The goal is to preserve enough context that a later change can be understood rather than mistaken for inconsistency.

## Decision record

### D-000 — Decision title

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Status:** Proposed / Accepted / Superseded / Rejected  
**Affected work:** Links to projects, concepts, or documents

#### Context

What condition, constraint, conflict, or opportunity required a decision?

#### Decision

What was chosen?

#### Reasons

Why was this direction selected over the alternatives?

#### Alternatives considered

What other approaches were materially considered, and why were they not selected?

#### Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, possible, or impossible because of this decision?

#### Evidence and assumptions

Which parts are supported by evidence, and which parts depend on assumptions that may later fail?

#### Revisit conditions

What new evidence, failure, dependency change, or threshold should trigger reconsideration?

#### Supersession

If replaced, link to the later decision and preserve this record unchanged except for its status and supersession link.
