# Threads

Threads are subjects that continue appearing across different times, contexts, projects, experiences, questions, or documents.

A thread does not need a conclusion. Its purpose is to keep related pieces from becoming isolated simply because they were recorded at different times.

## Thread index

| Thread | What keeps recurring | Connected entries | Current state |
|---|---|---|---|
| _None mapped yet_ | _Pattern, concern, idea, person, question, or subject_ | _Links_ | _Emerging / Active / Dormant / Resolved / Changed_ |

## Thread entry

### Thread name

**What this thread is:**  
A plain description of what keeps returning.

**Where it appears:**  
Links to thoughts, memories, projects, questions, experiences, references, or other entries.

**How it has changed:**  
Record shifts in meaning, interpretation, importance, or direction.

**Current tension:**  
What remains unresolved, contradictory, or difficult to place.

**Possible connection:**  
A relationship that may exist but has not yet been established.
